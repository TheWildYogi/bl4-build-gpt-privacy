# Privacy Policy — Borderlands 4 Builds GPT

Last updated: 2025-12-14

This GPT may use Actions to retrieve public information (such as official Borderlands 4 patch notes and announcements) from:
- borderlands.2k.com
- support.borderlands.com
- api.steampowered.com

## What data is processed
- The GPT may send parts of a user’s request to these public endpoints to fetch relevant patch/update content.
- No account login is used for these sources.

## What data is stored
- No personal data is intentionally collected or stored by this GPT.
- Any temporary logs (if any exist on the hosting provider) are used only for debugging and reliability.

## Sharing
- Data is not sold or shared with third parties.
